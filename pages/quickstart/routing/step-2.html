If you're using the CLI to follow along, you can complete this step by running the following command: ```sh lore tutorial step3 ``` ### Why make the title a link? Applications can grow to have many nested routes, and sometimes you just need a way to say "take me back to the homepage please!". You may not have consciously noticed it before, but the majority of applications provide this "escape" functionality by making the application logo or title in the header a link to the root route (typically `/`). When designing applications, it's nearly always a good idea to copy user experience patterns your users will already be familiar with.
